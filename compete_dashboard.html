Key Finding

Mindtrip dominates transactional and structured queries — flights, hotels, layovers, itineraries with budgets. The LLM judge rewarded actionability and specificity (flight numbers, prices, dates) heavily. Wanderboat wins on personalized/conversational queries where insight and reasoning matter more than data density. The judge also penalized Mindtrip's unverifiable numeric specifics on live-data queries.
